About Me

Rahul Saxena is an author, software developer, and instructor. Rahul was born and raised in India, where he completed his graduation and received a degree in computer science from Amity University in 2020.

Rahul is an enthusiast programmer who likes to participate in competitive programming and loves to build elegant websites using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 4, Javascript, & Angular. He stepped into coding when he was just 18 years old. He started his coding journey with the python programming language.

Rahul has one & half years of experience with large engineering projects focused on client's(MOFPI, FCI, SBI) needs to deliver quality products. His work experience in Tech Mahindra offers him a unique and global perspective.
